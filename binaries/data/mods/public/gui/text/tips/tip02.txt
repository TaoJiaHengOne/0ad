SPARTAN HOPLITES
Spartans are famous for their warriors and their last stand at the Battle of Thermopylae in 480 B.C.E., true to their code of honour and military training. The Spartan warriors began their lifelong training at age 7 and from then on his only occupation was war and the security of his state.

Spartiates are a Greek Poleis branch Super Infantry unit. They have high armour and attack, but are consequently slow and expensive. By researching the "Othismos" technology, the player commanding the Hellenes may array his Spartans into the deadly Phalanx formation.
